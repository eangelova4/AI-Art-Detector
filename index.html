AI Art Detector

What this tool does
AI tools have become extremely advanced, making it difficult to tell whether a piece of art is created by a human or generated by artificial intelligence. This website uses a Google Teachable Machine image classifier to analyze artwork directly in your browser.

How to use it

  1. Upload an image or enable your webcam.
  2. The model processes the image locally in your browser.
  3. You will see the probability and final result.
